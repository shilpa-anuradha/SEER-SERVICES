COMPANY PROFILE

OBJECTS


SEER SERVICES-SRI LANKA is assist in supply and procurement of Aircraft spare parts and other related components, equipment, accessories (Both of Rotary and Fixed Wing AIRCRAFT) to the Government as well as commercial Air lines in Sri Lanka in prominent way.

ABILITIES


SEER SERVICES is a firm, formed in Year 2010 by 2 expertise persons who had of extensive Aeronautical Engineering experience.
SEER SERVICES is able to carry out value chain development for the spares procurement between the principle suppliers to customers in the aviation entity.

BUSINESS CERTIFICATION


SEER SERVICES is certified by Sri Lanka Government Business act No 4 of 1990 under certificate No W/K/9025 in year 2010.
